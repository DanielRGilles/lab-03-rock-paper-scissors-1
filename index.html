Rock Paper Scissors

How to play: Pick rock, paper or scissors and click play. The computer will then pick an object and the winner will be determined as follows:

  • Rock beats scissors.
  • Scissors beats paper.
  • Paper beats rock.

If you and the computer pick the same object, it will count as a draw.
